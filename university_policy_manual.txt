UNIVERSITY - INSTITUTIONAL INVIGILATION & SCHEDULING GUIDELINES

MAXIMUM ASSIGNMENT LIMITS
To ensure academic quality and reduce proctor fatigue, no faculty member or invigilator may be assigned to more than 2 exam slots on any single academic day.

CONSECUTIVE SLOT SAFETY HOURS
An invigilator cannot be scheduled for consecutive timeslots (e.g., MON_FN followed immediately by MON_AN) unless there is a critical shortage. If a consecutive assignment is absolutely required, it must be assigned to Room R101 or R103, which have adjacent faculty rest lounges. Room R102 has no adjacent lounge and is strictly forbidden for consecutive invigilation.

VISITING FACULTY DESIGNATIONS
Prof. Sarah (FAC01) is registered as a Visiting Scholar. Visiting scholars are exempt from afternoon scheduling slots (AN) to facilitate local travel constraints. They must only be scheduled for forenoon sessions (FN).

ROOM CODES AND CAPACITIES

Room R101: Maximum seating limit of 30 students.

Room R102: Maximum seating limit of 40 students.

Room R103: Maximum seating limit of 120 students.
All examinations exceeding 50 registered candidates must be assigned to Room R103.
